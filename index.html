About Roosters Espresso

Roosters started as a neighborhood drive-thru for early-morning commuters. Today, it’s known for honest prices, strong coffee, and baristas who remember your order.

Quality

House-Roasted Beans

Roosters roasts its own beans, which means consistent flavor, fresher espresso, and a signature roast that sets them apart from big chains.

Value

Affordable Pricing

With lattes at around $4.25, Roosters stays below the Seattle average price while maintaining quality – important for daily coffee drinkers.

Convenience

Drive-Thru + I-5 Access

Both shops are located near main routes, making it easy for commuters to grab a drink without leaving their car or delaying their morning.

Data, Tags & KPIs

Behind the scenes, the site is set up for measurement. This allows Roosters to track what’s working and refine their marketing over time.

Tracking Setup

Data Collection

  • Google Tag Manager tags key actions: click-to-call, directions, menu views, bean purchases.
  • Google Analytics 4 records sessions, page views, traffic sources, and conversions.
  • DoorDash / Uber Eats / Grubhub dashboards track online order performance per location.
  • Meta Business Suite tracks social posts that drive traffic to the site.
KPIs

What Success Looks Like

  • Increase in monthly bean sales (online + in-store).
  • Growth in site traffic and pages per session.
  • Higher pre-order and delivery app volume for both locations.
  • Improved visibility for the Mountlake Terrace shop.
  • More reviews and repeat customers.
Reporting

How We Use the Data

  • Combine website, social, and delivery data in Excel or Tableau dashboards.
  • Identify top-performing drinks, promos, and channels.
  • Adjust content and ads based on real customer behavior.
